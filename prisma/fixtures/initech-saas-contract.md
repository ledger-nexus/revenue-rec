# Master Services Agreement

**Customer:** Initech Industries, Inc. ("Customer")
**Vendor:** Northwind Cloud Inc. ("Vendor")
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

---

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") governs the provision of cloud software and related implementation services by Vendor to Customer.

## 1. Subscription Services

Vendor will provide Customer with subscription access to Northwind Cloud Platform ("Platform") for the period of **January 1, 2026 through December 31, 2026** ("Subscription Term").

The Subscription includes:

- Unlimited user access to the Platform
- 24/7 standard support
- All Platform updates released during the Subscription Term

**Subscription Fee:** $5,000 per month, billed annually in advance.
**Total Subscription Fees:** $60,000 (USD)

## 2. Implementation Services

Vendor will provide one-time implementation services including data migration, configuration, and end-user training. Implementation services will be substantially completed by **March 31, 2026**.

**Implementation Fee:** $10,000 (one-time, USD)

## 3. Total Contract Value

The total amount payable by Customer under this Agreement is **$60,000 (USD)**, inclusive of all Subscription Fees and Implementation Services. *(Customer received a $10,000 discount on Implementation Services as a launch promotion.)*

## 4. Payment Terms

Total contract amount of $60,000 due upon Effective Date.

## 5. Term and Termination

This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for twelve (12) months. Either party may terminate upon written notice for material breach.
